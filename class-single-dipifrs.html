What is DipIFRS?

Diploma in International Financial Reporting Standards

DipIFRS is a short-term course offered by ACCA that equips students with the structure and understanding of the international accounting standards. It has become a benchmark for accounting professionals around the globe.

  • DipIFRS = Globally Accepted IFRS Diploma
  • DipIFRS Eligibility – Practising Professionals
  • DipIFRS Popularity – 3x Yearly Growth
  • DipIFRS Jobs Available – Big4's
  • DipIFRS Salary Range – Upto ₹8 Lac p.a.

DipIFRS Course Syllabus

The DipIFRS syllabus is structured in four modules, providing a comprehensive understanding of international financial reporting standards.

IFRS Module 1

International sources of authority and framework.

Topics Include:
  • International sources of authority
  • IFRS Framework
  • Conceptual Framework

IFRS Module 2

Elements of financial statements and presentation:

  • Elements of financial statements
  • Recognition and measurement
  • Presentation of financial statements
  • Additional disclosures

IFRS Module 3

Preparation of external financial reports for combined entities:

Topics Include:
  • Preparation of external financial reports
  • Combined entities
  • Associates
  • Joint arrangements
