FRM Course Syllabus

The FRM syllabus is divided into two parts covering 5 core topics. The first part focuses on understanding the foundations of risk management.

FRM Part 1

Foundation level covering essential risk management concepts and tools.

Topics Include:
  • Foundations of Risk Management
  • Quantitative Analysis
  • Financial Markets and Products
  • Valuations and Risk Models

FRM Part 2

Advanced level focusing on application of risk management tools and techniques:

  • Market Risk Measurement and Management
  • Credit Risk Measurement and Management
  • Operational and Integrated Risk Management
  • Risk Management and Investment Management
  • Current Issues in Financial Markets
